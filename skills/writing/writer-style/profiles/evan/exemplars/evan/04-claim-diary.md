# Exemplar — claim-diary mode

Index: claims-as-headings, quote/gloss, open gap. Domain-agnostic shape.

---

I read a 2013 paper that characterizes the design space with three properties.
These are my takeaways—not a product review.

## 1 — Three properties, not branding language

Path independence says the trader’s cost does not depend on splitting one trade
into many. Translation invariance shrinks spreads toward zero for the LP.
Liquidity sensitivity ties price impact to activity.

## 2 — You only get two of three

No pricing rule is translation invariant, path independent, **and** liquidity
sensitive. The classical construction gets the first two and gives up the third.

## 3 — Open question left open

How does this map cleanly onto the fee-bearing case used in practice? I could not
find a direct bridge in the literature I checked. Closest pointers still leave a
gap—so it stays open.
