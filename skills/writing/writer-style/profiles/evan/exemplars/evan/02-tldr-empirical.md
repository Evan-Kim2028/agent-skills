# TLDR

* Pyth contract has a **26.5%** tx failure rate (292,165 txs) across 1.1m
  transactions, **1.45 SOL** in lost fees — cross-checked against Dune and Solscan.
* Network-wide failure rate in the window is **8%**, **346 SOL** in lost fees.
* Pyth runs **~3.3×** the general failure rate.
* Failed fees still pay validators: there is little economic incentive to drive
  failure rates to zero even when blockspace is “wasted.”
